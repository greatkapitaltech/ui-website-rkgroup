1980

The Beginning

A small fabric trading shop in Chikpet - started by a young R K Shah, coming to a nascent metropolitan city from Adoni. His early years of textile trade were challenging, having to break existing market clout to build space for his work from scratch. Many challenges and 9 tough years later he finally saw tides turn after trying many different avenues of business.

1990

Pioneering Textile Imports & Retail Expansion

A true pioneer, in the early 90s he started to explore textile trade opportunities outside of India. In 1996, he was the first to import textiles to India from premium textile manufacturing mills across the globe and there was no looking back. Constantly striving to grow, a cold call in early 1998 led him to Coats Viyella, UK, and eventually to RK Group's first large-scale partnership setting up 75 Peter England stores.

2000

New Millennium, New Ventures

As operations grew, with an established network of textile mills across the globe, he identified opportunity in the upcoming mobile phone industry. Strengthening his business foundations at HBS also led to building a network of cross learning and support in the form of the HBS angels - mentoring, supporting and growing entrepreneurship.

2010

E-Commerce Revolution Begins

2013 saw the beginning of what was to become a long & fruitful partnership with Amazon - becoming the first Organised Seller supplying over 300 brands to Cloud Tail. 2014: RK Group gained exclusive distribution and import rights for Xiaomi online. 2018: Partnership with Flipkart established, marking expansion across multiple e-commerce platforms.

2020

Cross-Border Trade & Multi-Platform Expansion

2019 marked the initiation of ValueKart bringing in marquee international brands to the Indian e-commerce market. 2020: Took charge of ABFRL distribution across Ajio, Nykaa, Myntra, Amazon and other platforms, solidifying RK Group's position as a multi-platform e-commerce powerhouse.

2021

Fintech & Automotive Ventures

2021: Partnered with Cars24 to deliver a first-of-its-kind online experience in the Indian pre-owned automobiles industry. 2021: Great Kapital was established to revolutionize working capital management through innovative supply chain finance solutions.

2022

FMCG Market Leadership

RK World Infocom established as the largest FMCG seller on Amazon, built on trust and partnership excellence. Achieving unprecedented scale in year one, solidifying RK Group's position as India's leading FMCG distribution platform.

2024

Quick Commerce Dominance

Became South India's exclusive partner for Blinkit, establishing a stronghold in the quick commerce sector. Expanding our reach and capabilities in the rapidly growing instant delivery market across South India.

2025

Strategic Brand Partnerships & Healthcare Expansion

Robust Kommerce partners with global brands including Nike on Myntra, elevating premium brand presence in India. Expanding into healthcare e-commerce through PharmEasy partnership. Launching innovative fintech solutions with Scapia Federal Bank, diversifying into new-age financial services.
